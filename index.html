Take a step into my world

I'm a student Graphic en digital media at AP hogeschool Antwerp. I'm 19 years old, but full of adventure.

As you can see I really adore street art such as grafitit. I've seen this kind of streetart with my own eyes in Melbourne, Australia and Berlin, Germany.

Beside streetart I have a passion for travelling, I started when I was 17 years old with travelling to Australia for 7 months and to California for 2 months. Since it has been an amazing and once in a lifetime experience, I need to see more and more.

I travelled with an organisation called EF You should check it out!

Something you should also know about me is that I love quotes. Quotes often express in words how I feel when I can't and I think that's amazing.
